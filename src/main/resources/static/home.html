Our Story

A Journey Built
on Passion

Furn began in a small workshop in Chennai in 2018. Our founder, a third-generation woodworker, believed that the art of crafting real wooden furniture was being lost to cheap alternatives and mass production.

With just three craftsmen and a handful of tools, we took our first order — a Pooja unit for a family in Tambaram. The pride they felt when it was installed became the reason Furn exists today.

"We don't just make furniture. We make the backdrop to your family's most important moments."

Today, Furn has grown to serve over 500 homes across Chennai, with a team of 20+ skilled artisans. But our soul has never changed — every unit is still built by hand, with the same care and precision as that very first Pooja unit.
